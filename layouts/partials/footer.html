About Thinking Atoms

Through Thinking Atoms, I aim to combine my love for science with my other passions and connect with like-minded individuals. The blog has four sections: Popular Science, where I simplify the chemistry behind everyday life; Think Box, a space for my reflections on life and personal lessons; Poems, featuring my Hindi poetry that expresses emotions and stories; and Hindi Corner, where I share my thoughts in Hindi, my mother tongue, for genuine expression.
